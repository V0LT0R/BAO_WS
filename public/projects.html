Main products:

"Creation of a biotechnological system - a filter for air purification in the surrounding area using Internet of Things technologies and the ability to control the level of air pollution in the city"

"Creation of a biotechnological system - a filter for air purification in the surrounding area using Internet of Things technologies and the ability to control the level of air pollution in the city" The main idea is to improve air quality in the city of Nur-Sultan through the development and installation of a working prototype of a biotechnological system - a filter for purification air in the selected area using Internet of Things (IoT) technologies and further monitoring of air pollution levels in real time through an information system (portal) using data analysis and visualization tools.

Development of a number of software complexes for the Ministry of Education and Science of Ukraine

Such as: "System of support and control of state awards MES of Ukraine", "The system of accounting for the index of citation of scientific and pedagogical cadres of Ukraine", "Creation of a system to verify the degree of uniqueness of scientific works "Anti-plagiarism" Basic characteristics, development content: The check system of scientific papers uniqueness has been created. The basis of the system encharged on the following scientific results: the method of determination coincidences in texts of electronic documents; method of filtering of false matches; method of documents clustering with the use of words frequency in the text (TF-measure); method of text canonization using morphological Ukrainian dictionary, dictionaries of synonyms and antonyms.

The global positioning (GPS), dispatching and control system "Inspector" is designed for monitoring, control and management of the vehicle fleet.

"Inspector" is used in car fleets, taxis, farms, construction and special equipment fleet, emergency dispatching offices, management of sales agents and managers or simply for private control of a vehicle, and also, for the safety of children, relatives, pets and VIPs. The universal controller can be used in various fields, for example: Municipal services (smart house, smart city, smart transport, energy management, etc.) Production (control and management of equipment, warehouse logistics, etc.) Transport sector (various types of transport and additional equipment management) Environment (control of water level in rivers, control of air pollution, etc.) Private sector (smart house). Agriculture (transport control, monitoring and prediction of weather conditions, irrigation management, precision farming and ICT farming), Etc.
